Why Hybrid Mobile App?

The days that people could argue that hybrid apps are too slow and not responsive are over. In the past few years hybrid apps have come a long way and are just as fast and responsive as a native app. Not only that, but hybrid apps allow you to build for multiple platforms on a single code base, making them more economical for many businesses. 

From Geolocation, BlueTooth iBeacons, and a host of other features allow you to give your customers and clients what they want and need for your business to succeed in a world quickly being dominated by mobile devices.

iBeacons

App users can interact without having to do anything. iBeacons let the app know when it is in a certain area and show data based on that. For example, when the user enters an area of the business it can show a sale item or information on that area.

Geolocation

Being aware of the users location is a hot item now a days. Like iBeacons you can read the geolocation of the user and show data or information based on that location.

Social Sharing

Sharing to social media is a big part of many peoples lives, LinkedIn, Twitter and especially Facebook are used on a daily basis as not just a means to keep in touch with friends and family, but is a viable means of eCommerce, both big and small. 

Push Notifications

When used correctly push notifications, both local and server based can be a great way to let you users know of specials or other items of interest.
